Our Holistic Approach

In our clinic we blend medical eye care with simple wellness tools: screen-time planning, posture and lighting advice, lifestyle coaching for dry eye, and relaxation techniques you can do at home.

During your visit we listen to your daily routine—work, sleep, study, and device use—then create a realistic plan that fits your life, not the other way around.
