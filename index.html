About Tee Opticals

Welcome to Tee Opticals - Your Complete Eyecare Solution.

At Tee Opticals, we are a complete eyecare brand combining medical expertise with modern style. Under our brand, we operate two specialized divisions:

Tee Optometry Clinic - for professional eye exams, clinical management, and vision therapy, and Tee Opticals Retail - for prescription glasses, sunglasses, contact lenses, and stylish frames.

Our experienced team provides comprehensive vision solutions that prioritize both your eye health and personal style. We believe that clear vision is essential to living your best life, and we're here to help you achieve that.

Our Services

Complete eyecare solutions combining medical expertise with modern style

Tee Optometry Clinic

Professional Eye Care You Can Trust

Our clinic is dedicated to protecting your eye health through expert consultations, accurate eye tests, and evidence-based treatments. Whether you need a routine check-up, management for eye conditions, or advice on visual comfort, our optometry team is here for you.

Comprehensive Eye Exams

Thorough eye examinations using state-of-the-art equipment to assess your vision health and detect any potential issues early.

Disease Screening

Screening for glaucoma, cataracts, and retinal disease to ensure early detection and proper management.

Children's Vision Care

Specialized care for children's vision and amblyopia treatment to ensure healthy visual development.

Digital Eye Strain

Dry eye and digital eye strain management for modern lifestyle comfort and visual wellness.

Tee Opticals Retail

Style Meets Vision

From prescription lenses to sunglasses and blue-light blockers, Tee Opticals offers eyewear that combines quality, comfort, and fashion. Our optical team ensures every pair is fitted for your needs and lifestyle.

Prescription Frames

Stylish prescription frames that combine functionality with fashion, tailored to your vision needs and personal style.

Sunglasses

UV-protected and polarized sunglasses to protect your eyes while keeping you stylish in any setting.

Contact Lenses

Expert contact lens fitting and consultation services to find the perfect lenses for your lifestyle and comfort needs.

Blue-Light Protection

Blue-light blocking and transition lenses to protect your eyes from digital strain and changing light conditions.
