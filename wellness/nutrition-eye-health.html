Nutrition for Eye Health

What you eat can help protect against cataracts, macular degeneration and dry eye. Small changes in diet go a long way.

Nutrients your eyes love

  • Omega‑3s (from oily fish, flaxseed, walnuts) support the tear film and retina.
  • Lutein & zeaxanthin (from dark leafy greens) protect the macula.
  • Vitamin C & E (from fruits, nuts and seeds) act as antioxidants.
  • Zinc (from beans, nuts, whole grains) supports retinal health.

Balanced lifestyle

A colourful plate, not smoking, healthy weight and good blood sugar control all play a role in maintaining healthy eyes through life.

During your visit

We can discuss simple, realistic nutrition tweaks that fit your culture, budget and routine – no fad diets required.
