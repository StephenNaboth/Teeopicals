Eye Wellness & Prevention

Wellness means protecting your eyes before problems start – through smart habits, environment and regular checks.

Everyday wellness habits

  • Follow the 20–20–20 rule for screens: every 20 minutes, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  • Keep screens slightly below eye level and at a comfortable arm’s length.
  • Use proper lighting and avoid glare when reading or working.
  • Wear UV‑blocking sunglasses outdoors.

Regular preventative checks

Even without symptoms, routine eye exams are key to detecting silent problems such as glaucoma, diabetic eye disease or early cataract.

Build your wellness plan

During your visit we can help you create a realistic eye wellness plan tailored to your work, devices and sleep pattern.
