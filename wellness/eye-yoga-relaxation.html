Eye Yoga & Relaxation

Simple, evidence‑informed relaxation practices to ease visual tension, especially for people who work or study for long hours.

What is “eye yoga”?

We use this term to describe gentle exercises: palming, guided blinking, focus shifting and breathing techniques that help relax the muscles around the eyes and face.

How we use it

During wellness sessions we can show you a short routine that fits into work breaks or evening wind‑down, supporting both visual comfort and general relaxation.

Important note

Eye yoga and relaxation support comfort, but do not replace proper medical eye exams for disease detection or treatment.
