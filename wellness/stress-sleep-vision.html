Stress, Sleep & Vision

Eye comfort is closely linked to your overall stress level and sleep quality. We take these into account when planning your care.

How stress shows up in your eyes

Stress can trigger eye strain, headaches, twitching eyelids and difficulty focusing, especially when combined with long hours on screens and poor posture.

The role of sleep

Short or poor‑quality sleep can worsen dry eye, slow healing and reduce your tolerance for contact lenses or long visual tasks.

What we discuss

During a wellness visit we may ask about your work pattern, stress and sleep, then suggest small, realistic changes and relaxation techniques that support both your vision and wellbeing.
