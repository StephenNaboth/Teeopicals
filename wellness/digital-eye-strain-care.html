Digital Eye Strain Care

Long hours on screens can cause tired, sore eyes and headaches. We help you protect your vision in a digital world.

Symptoms of digital eye strain

Common symptoms include tired eyes, burning or dryness, blurred vision at the end of the day, neck and shoulder pain or headaches after using devices.

Our approach

We review your screen set‑up, working distance, posture, blink rate and lighting, then recommend a mix of wellness strategies, lubricants and, if needed, specialised computer lenses or blue‑light filters.

Practical tips

  • Use the 20–20–20 rule and remember to blink fully.
  • Keep screens slightly below eye level.
  • Consider dedicated computer glasses if you work on screens many hours a day.
