When to See Us

Visit Teeopticals if you notice sudden blurred vision, eye pain, flashes or floaters, headaches with screen use, or if it has been more than one year since your last eye exam. Early checks mean better outcomes.

You can book directly with our clinic—no referral needed. If we detect a condition that needs surgery or specialist input, we arrange a trusted referral and guide you through every step.
