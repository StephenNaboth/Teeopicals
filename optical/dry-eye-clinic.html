Dry Eye Clinic

Burning, gritty, watery eyes or discomfort at the end of the day may be signs of dry eye. Our clinic provides a full assessment and tailored treatment plan.

Dry eye assessment

We look at your tear film quality, blink pattern, meibomian glands and lifestyle factors such as screen time, medications and environment. This helps us identify the main cause of your symptoms.

Treatment options

Management may include lubricating drops, warm compresses, lid hygiene, in‑office treatments and lifestyle adjustments. We create a simple daily plan you can realistically follow.

When to seek help

Book a dry eye consultation if your eyes feel tired, sandy, watery or uncomfortable with reading or computer work.
