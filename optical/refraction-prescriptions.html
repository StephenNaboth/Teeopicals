Refraction & Prescriptions

Refraction is the part of the exam where we fine‑tune your glasses power for crisp, comfortable vision at distance and near.

How we find your prescription

We combine objective measurements (such as autorefractor or retinoscopy) with your responses to “which is clearer, one or two?” to arrive at a prescription that balances clarity and comfort.

Understanding your script

After the exam we explain what your numbers mean – short‑sighted, long‑sighted, astigmatism or presbyopia – and how different lens designs can support your work, driving and reading needs.

Next steps

You can use your prescription at our optical shop or elsewhere. We are happy to review any concerns if your new glasses do not feel quite right.
