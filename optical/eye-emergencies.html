Eye Emergencies

Sudden red eye, pain, loss of vision or trauma should never be ignored. Teeopticals offers urgent assessment and fast referral when needed.

Symptoms that need urgent attention

Please contact us or seek emergency care immediately if you experience:

  • Sudden loss or shadow over vision.
  • Severe eye pain, light sensitivity or headache.
  • Flashes of light, many new floaters or a curtain in your vision.
  • Chemical splash, penetrating injury or trauma to the eye.

We will assess your eyes, provide first‑line management and arrange rapid referral to the appropriate hospital or specialist if required.
