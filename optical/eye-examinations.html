Eye Examinations

A comprehensive eye exam at Teeopticals checks more than just your glasses power – we assess eye health, visual comfort, and risks for future disease.

What happens during an eye exam?

Your appointment includes a detailed history, vision and refraction testing, eye pressure checks, and a full health assessment of the front and back of the eye. We explain each step in simple language and answer your questions.

How often should I test my eyes?

Most adults benefit from an eye test every 1–2 years. Children, contact lens wearers, people with diabetes, high glasses power or a family history of eye disease may need more frequent review.

Good to know

  • Bring your current glasses and any eye medications.
  • We may dilate your pupils for a better view of the retina.
  • We provide a written prescription and clear plan after every exam.
