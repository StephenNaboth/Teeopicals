Cataract Assessment & Referral

We help you understand if cataracts are affecting your vision and guide you through the decision and referral for surgery.

Signs cataracts may be developing

Common symptoms include cloudy or dim vision, glare from headlights, difficulty seeing at night, and frequent changes in your glasses prescription. We assess how much cataracts are impacting your day‑to‑day life.

Our role in your cataract journey

Teeopticals provides the initial assessment, explains lens options in simple terms, then arranges referral to a trusted surgeon. After surgery we check healing and update your prescription.

When to book

If you notice glare, difficulty driving at night or reading, or a recent drop in vision, schedule a cataract assessment to discuss your options early.
