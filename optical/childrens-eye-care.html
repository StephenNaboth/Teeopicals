Children’s Eye Care

Good vision is essential for learning and development. We provide friendly, child‑focused eye exams and follow‑up care.

What we check

We assess vision in each eye, eye alignment (squint), depth perception, focusing ability and eye health. Where needed we investigate for lazy eye (amblyopia) or high myopia and create a treatment plan.

School and home signs to watch for

Squinting, sitting very close to the TV, rubbing eyes, headaches, poor reading or avoidance of near work can all be clues that a child is struggling with their vision.

When to book

Children should have an eye check before starting school and regularly through their school years, even if they do not complain of problems.
