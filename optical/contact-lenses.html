Contact Lenses

From occasional wear to everyday use, we help you choose comfortable, healthy contact lenses that fit your lifestyle.

Fitting and training

Your appointment includes an eye health check, lens selection, and hands‑on training for insertion, removal and care. We only finalise your prescription once you are confident and comfortable.

Types of lenses

We offer daily, fortnightly and monthly lenses, options for astigmatism and presbyopia, and lenses for sports or cosmetic use. We will recommend what is safest and most practical for you.

Aftercare

Regular aftercare visits help keep your eyes healthy and catch early signs of dryness or redness linked to lens wear.
