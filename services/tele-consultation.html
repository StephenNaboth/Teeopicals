Tele‑consultation

For suitable problems, we offer remote follow‑up and advice by phone or video, reducing the need to travel to the clinic.

When tele‑consultation can help

Tele‑consultations are useful for discussing test results, monitoring stable conditions, checking how you are coping with new lenses, or answering questions about treatment and wellness plans.

Certain emergencies and first‑time assessments will still require an in‑person visit – our team will guide you.

How to book

Contact us by phone, WhatsApp or email and request a tele‑consultation. We will advise on fees and schedule a time.
